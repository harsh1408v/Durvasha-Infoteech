GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE MANAGEMENT

Stand out in the

local pack

Local SEO starts with a fully optimized and managed Google
Business Profile (formerly known as Google My Business). We
understand Google’s local algorithm and study which features of
your Google Business Profile can influence your rankings and
conversions.

Your dedicated account manager will work with you to
understand your business goals and create a local SEO strategy
that’s right for you. We will optimize your Profile with accurate
information, publish fresh weekly Google Posts, feature your
products and services, develop a review strategy, keep tabs on
your competitors, and send you a monthly report.

REVIEW STRATEGY & MANAGEMENT

WEBSITE OPTIMIZATION

Rank higher & convert

more leads

Every business is unique and so are our website optimization
strategies. We start with an audit of your website, analyzing
everything that affects your ability to rank on Google. You’ll get a
full report from our audit including every area of improvement
listed by priority, which then becomes our task list.

We’ll optimize your website, starting with the top priorities to
increase your online conversions and working through our full list
of recommendations. Our custom strategies will get you results:
increased engagement, traffic, conversions, and sales.

LINK BUILDING

Boost rankings faster

Link building is a slow and difficult process for many businesses,
yet the quality and authority of inbound links to a website domain
is the #1 local organic ranking factor. Our link building service
can improve your business’s visibility on Google search by
increasing prominence – a pillar of Google’s local algorithm.

We create in-content editorial backlinks in fresh posts on blogs
that have real organic traffic. We write relevant and high quality
content that mentions your business, then we pitch that content
to blogs related to your business category. Every month,
we’ll send you the link to a new post that mentions your business and
links to your website.

*Link pricing may vary for certain industries. Your account
manager will review pricing with you before billing.
