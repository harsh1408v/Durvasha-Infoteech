LINK BUILDING

Boost rankings faster

Link building is a slow and difficult process for many businesses,
yet the quality and authority of inbound links to a website domain
is the #1 local organic ranking factor. Our link building service
can improve your business’s visibility on Google search by
increasing prominence – a pillar of Google’s local algorithm.

We create in-content editorial backlinks in fresh posts on blogs
that have real organic traffic. We write relevant and high quality
content that mentions your business, then we pitch that content
to blogs related to your business category. Every month,
we’ll send you the link to a new post that mentions your business and
links to your website.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

Our SEO Services are designed for single, multi-location, and enterprise businesses. Our services are perfect for business owners and decision-makers who don’t have time to master Google Business Profiles, but know the value of having an expert on their team. We love to work with home services, law firms, dentists, medical professionals, retail and enterprise businesses. Our clients that get the best results understand that investing a little of your time helps us make a greater impact, and (as with anything) results take time.

Can't I just manage my own Google Business Profile?

You can manage your business profile, but ask yourself “how much is my time worth?” Staying up to date on Google’s changing guidelines, new feature releases and constant bugs is a full-time job, not to mention the time it takes to keep your business profile optimized and ranking. Whitespark’s local SEO experts will make sure your business is showing up in search results and converting searchers into customers, so you can focus on serving all of your new customers and watching your business grow! Our clients enjoy an 87% increase in phone calls, 567% increase in reviews, 29% increase in keywords ranking in the top 3 spots, and 2x website clicks, on average.

This sounds great, but will I get qualified leads?

For our Google Business Profile Management Service, we measure calls, website visits and direction requests from your Google Business Profile every month so that you know how many customers are engaging with your business. This service also helps you manage and improve your reputation and promote your brand; both are incredibly beneficial and can improve conversion rate, but are more difficult to quantify. For our Website Management Service, we optimize your website to be found by searchers and we measure calls, contact form submissions and more – we customize this service to your business goals.
Read one of our case studies to see a real client’s results!

Do you charge a setup fee?

Heck no, we don’t! While most agencies will charge a set up fee for services like ours, that’s not how we roll. We know that in the first month of service, setting up your campaign for success requires more time, planning, and extra hours, but we will not charge you more in the first month. We are dedicated to providing you with the best service and taking your Business Profile to the next level. We’ve got you!
